Trip to Bali


Last holiday I had a vacation with my family. My father and I decided to go to Bali. We went to Bali by plane, but after I arrived in Bali we took the trip by car.

We spent there for 6 days. We stayed at our resort during the trip. We visited many tourism spots. We visited Besakih Temple, Sanur and Kuta beaches. We also visited Tampaskiring and of course we never forget to go shopping in the most well known Shopping center in Bali, Joger.

That was a very marvelous trip I ever did, even after we went home we are so tired but its fun.



Answer these questions based on text above.



1. What is the type of the text above?

2. Who was going to Bali?

3. When did they go to Bali?

4. How many days did the writer spent the holiday?

5. How did the writer feel?


Answers:



1. Recount text

2. The writer and the writer’s/his/her family

3. Last holiday

4. Six days

5. The writer/he/she felt happy
