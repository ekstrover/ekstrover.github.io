WRITING INVITATION



In writing an invitation to a birthday party, there are several things that you need to write on your invitation, they are:


- The place the party will be held
- The date the party will be held
- The time the party will start
- Party concept and dress code
- How to get there

Remember that in writing an invitation and your future plans, please use present continous tense.
