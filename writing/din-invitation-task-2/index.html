1. Write an invitation based on the situation given.

2. SITUATION

It is your birthday next Monday and you are having a party. You want to invite your friend. Write an invitation to her/him. Remember to include these things in your invitation, they are:

- The place the party will be held
- The date the party will be held
- The time the party will start
- Party concept and dress code
- How to get there
